Here we are going to discuss about different kinds of cat breeds.
There are 71 cat breeds recognized by The International Cat Association. These 71 breeds do not include the average houshold cat,
typically known as the domestic shorthair.
Every cat breed is different from another. Some cats have long fluffy fur and some cats don't have fur at all.

  2. Devon Rex
  3. Kurilian Bobtail
